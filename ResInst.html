- Recent Research Projects -

Ethical Implications of Brain-Computer Interfaces

Investigating the ethical concerns surrounding the development and use of BCIs, particularly regarding privacy, consent, and the potential for mind manipulation.

Ethics of Artificial Intelligence in Neuroscience

Exploring the ethical issues related to the integration of AI in neuroscience research and clinical applications, including decision-making processes and the potential biases in AI algorithms.

Ethical Use of Neurogenetics

Examining the ethical issues related to the use of genetic information in neuroscience, including implications for identity, privacy, and the potential for genetic enhancement.

Neuroethics of Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS)

Studying the long-term effects and ethical implications of using DBS for treating neurological and psychiatric conditions, such as Parkinson's disease and depression.
